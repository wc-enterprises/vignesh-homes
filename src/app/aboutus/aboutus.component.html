About Us
To give you a straightforward answer, we're the engineers, architects, designers, and electricians who build the foundation of innumerable dreams. But first and foremost, we are ordinary people - people who have lived in, admired, and dreamt of owning a home just like you. And that's why at Vignesh Homes, we often ponder over a very fundamental but certainly a crucial question - What makes a home? It was in seeking the answer to this question that a beautiful dream like Vignesh Homes shaped into reality and was incepted in 2003 by S.Karthikeyan. What began with just bricks and cement paved the way for a realization that "Homes are the people who live in it." Ever since, it was crystal clear that it is through listening intently and understanding their views that we can tap into the ideal home that lies behind their heart and mind. At Vignesh homes, the customer is king. Our journey begins with a thorough articulation of their requisites and then transcending the limitations of innovation and design. We aspire to build homes that feel home with unmatched capabilities in terms of manufacturing, engineering, and technology. Three years into the field, and we're climbing the ladder up faster than the industry has ever known before with a team empowered by unprecedented strength, knowledge, and perseverance. Headquartered in Chennai, we have firmly established ourselves, imprinting an exceptional mark in delivering residential, hospitality, and commercial projects as the leading developers who realize the dreams of our esteemed clients. Today, Vignesh Homes is synonymous with building the dreams of Tamil Nadu and turning homes into happiness in style because we know what matters the most.
